Zhenhui Peng is an associate professor in the School of Artificial Intelligence, Sun Yat-sen University. Before that, he was a postdoctoral researcher in Aalto University, working with Prof. Antti Oulasvirta. He got his Ph.D. degree in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, supervised by Prof. Xiaojuan Ma. He received his Bachelor degree in ESE from Nanjing University. Zhenhui leads the HaiA (Human-AI Augmentation) lab, which aims at augmenting human intelligence in higher-order thinking activities (e.g., critical/creative thinking, decision making, problem solving) with user-generated content (e.g., posts and comments on social media) and AI-generated content. The application domains include language learning, art education, mental health peer support, news/paper reading, and so on.

We welcome postdocs, undergraduate/graduate students, visiting interns, and scholars. Please send me your CV if you are interested in these topics or have any question.
