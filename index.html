Biography

My name is Zhenhui Peng (彭振辉). I am a fourth-year PhD student majored in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) in department of Computer Science and Engineering(CSE), Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). My supervisor is Dr. Xiaojuan Ma. Before that, I got my Bachelor degree in ESE from Nanjing University in 2017. My current research interests are in the design and evaluation of human-agent interaction (e.g., Human-Robot Interaction, chatbot / bot design), analysis of textual data on social media (e.g., online mental health community), and visualization.
