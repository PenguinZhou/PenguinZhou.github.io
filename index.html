Biography

I am a PhD student (Since Fall 2017) in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) in Hong Kong Univeristy of Science and Technology (HKUST). I major in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) supervised by Dr. Xiaojuan Ma. I got my Bachelor degree in ESE from Nanjing University in 2017. My current research interests are in Human-Robot Interaction, chatbot design and Reinforcement Learning. I welcome all the opportunities for collaboration and sharing.
